# Hide Or Die

> A multiplayer hide-and-seek horror game set inside a arcade.

---

## ABOUT THE GAME

Hide Or Die is a multiplayer social horror game where one player becomes the Seeker while the others must survive by hiding, moving, and managing their boredom before time runs out.

The arcade is filled with loud machines, dark corners, lockers, and hiding spots — but staying hidden forever is impossible.

To survive, hiders must interact with arcade machines to refill their boredom meter, which creats noise that reveals their location to the Seeker.

Meanwhile, the Seeker hunts players using sound, proximity voice chat, and careful map awareness.

---

## CORE FEATURES

### Hiders
- Hide inside the arcade
- Manage a boredom meter
- Refill boredom using arcade machines
- Place flash mines
- Fake machine sounds to distract the Seeker
- Use proximity voice chat to communicate

### Seeker
- Uses a minimap and sound to track players
- Higher stamina than hiders
- Can open lockers and hiding spots
- Uses a close-range shotgun to eliminate players

---

## GAMEPLAY LOOP

1. Players spawn into the arcade
2. A random player becomes the Seeker
3. Hiders scatter and hide
4. Boredom slowly drains over time
5. Hiders must risk making noise to survive
6. The Seeker tracks sounds and hunts players
7. Active arcade machines become limited as the round progresses
8. Survive until the timer ends or eliminate every hider
